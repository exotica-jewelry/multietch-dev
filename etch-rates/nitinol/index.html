Etching NiTinol with Multi‑Etch®

How to etch NiTinol with Multi‑Etch®, the safer alternative to hydrofluoric acid

Multi-Etch was originally developed for use on titanium, and is a perfect low-acid (pH 6.8) alternative to hazardous hydrofluoric (HF) acid for etching NiTinol

NiTinol, or nickel titanium, is an alloy of roughly equal amounts of the elements. It exhibits shape memory and pseudoelasticity.
